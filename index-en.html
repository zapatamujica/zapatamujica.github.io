Juan Sebastián Zapata-Mujica

Social researcher interested in the problem of the historical development of human thought. Photographer.

Current Projects

  • 🎥 Las Señoritas Documentary
    Since 2021, I have been working with Santiago Dussan, Daniel Velásquez, Daniela Rocha, Santiago Forero, Mamo Bernardo Torres, and the collaboration of many people on the production of this documentary. Currently (2025), we dedicate six hours a week to the post-production of the film. We have a first cut of the introduction, which, in its raw form, is about 35 minutes long. We are working on the second cut.

    You can find more information online with the hashtag #LasSeñoritasDocumental, and you can also watch this teaser on YouTube, a behind the scenes of the first part of the shoot. More information can also be found in the interviews and scientific journals sections.

Some of my photographs have been published by El Tiempo, Semanario Voz, and Cartel Urbano.
